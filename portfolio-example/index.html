About me

A bit of background.

I've spent the last six years working at the intersection of design and engineering. I started in graphic design, moved into UI/UX, and eventually taught myself to build the things I was designing.

These days I focus on design systems, data visualization, and frontend architecture. I care about accessibility, performance, and writing code that other people can read.

When I'm not at a screen, you'll find me trail running, reading science fiction, or experimenting with fermentation.
